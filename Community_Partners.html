Community Partners

Lower Merion School District

In December 2014, ten Welsh Valley students visited Ardmore House to come up with a solution to the residents' trouble with an ill-equipped television and a game storage unit that was very low to the ground and difficult for residents to use. The students designed and built a beautiful and functional two-foot high cabinet on wheels made of maple wood with a mahogany trim, which would support the storage unit making it easily accessible for all of the residents.
